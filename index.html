Story Bank

These are the proof stories Gemini uses as Paragraph 2 in every cover letter. Write them yourself — 50–65 words, first person, specific result. AI picks the best match per job.

Formula: "At [company], I [did what] — [with whom / under what constraint]. The result was [specific, ideally measurable outcome]."
Aim for 6–10 stories covering: product launch, entrepreneurship, cross-functional work, research, international exposure, business impact.
